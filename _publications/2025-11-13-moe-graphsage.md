We propose a MoE-GraphSAGE-based framework that enables integrated evaluation of transient rotor angle and voltage stability in power systems, leveraging graph neural networks with mixture-of-experts architecture for enhanced stability assessment.

**Authors:** **Kunyu Zhang**, Guang Yang, Fashun Shi, Shaoying He, Yuchi Zhang
